Data Backup

Selecting the reach of your prospective backup system is one of many choices you need to make when you plan to backup your data. Numerous adverse events, such as cyber-attacks, cyber fraud, physical theft, hardware, and software failure, may occur on a device, server or network. With our data backup services, you can select Disks or tape backup, Hybrid cloud backup. or Direct-to-cloud backup option, best suited for your requirement. You can choose from varieties of backup options like Full Backup, Incremental Backup, Differential Backup, Mirror Backup, Local Backup, Offsite Backup and so on.
✓ Reliable backup process
✓ Enterprise wide, single access backup system

Disaster Recovery

For business continuity and IT security, it’s always advised to link your data backup systems with a disaster recovery plan. Many organizations get confused with the backup but not realize it’s much more than that. Our Disaster recovery services will help you to address most critical questions like determining application priority, aligning your backup policy with your DR strategy, assigning RTOs and RPOs, creating a testing and verification protocol and many more.
✓ Compressive recovery management
✓ Little or no downtime
✓ Effective and uncomplicated recovery timeline
